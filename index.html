Abstract

We present VietSpeechLLM-TTS, a scalable and multilingual-friendly speech language model tailored for the Vietnamese domain. Our system supports multi-speaker synthesis with the unique capability of dynamically selecting speaker identity based on text semantics, style, or intent â€” without explicit speaker prompts. By conditioning speech generation on both linguistic and latent semantic cues, the model adapts its prosody and speaker characteristics in a context-aware manner. While focused on Vietnamese, our approach is architecturally extensible to other low-resource or code-switching language scenarios. We demonstrate natural and diverse synthesized speech across various voices and registers, and showcase the potential of Speech LLMs in controllable, language-adaptive text-to-speech.
